About Us

Redemption is a skilled, innovative team specializing in wood-focused, investment furniture that enhances indoor and outdoor spaces. With over a decade of experience, they design and craft unique statement pieces for individuals, homes, restaurants, lodges, and hotels. Based in Centurion, South Africa, they distribute across Gauteng and beyond. Their vision is to transform nature’s beauty into functional, eye-catching furniture, inspired by Psalms 107 and John 1:1-4.
